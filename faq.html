Your Questions Answered.
What is Memry?
Memry is a comprehensive platform that serves as a hub for users to cultivate and enhance their authentic relationships.
Is Memry safe?
Since the start, Memry has been designed with user safety as a paramount priority.
Where can I get support?
Support is available at all times. Just reach out using the chat feature or visit support page on the website.
Is my information safe?
Rest assured, your sensitive user data remains safeguarded and is never shared with any third-party partner.
Can random people access my information?
Only the friends you personally accept and choose can view the content you share on Memry.
How often are updates released?
Memry works to provide you with meaningful updates regularly, ensuring you have the latest and greatest features.
How do I enable notifications?
Navigate to 'Settings', find 'Notifications', and enable them to stay informed on all things related to Memry!
